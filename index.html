Swiftian: Think Swift

Welcome to the Swiftian repository! This project powers swiftian.com, the home for an innovative iPhone coding app designed to empower you to learn Swift while building your own productivity apps.

What is Swiftian?

Swiftian is a unique mini IDE built around Swift’s syntax. It’s not only a place to learn Swift coding in a hands-on way, but also a platform to create apps directly from your iPhone. Whether you’re a beginner or looking to boost your coding skills, Swiftian lets you:

  • Learn Swift Coding: Dive into Swift programming with an interactive, real-world approach.
  • Code on the Go: Enjoy the flexibility of writing code anytime, anywhere from your mobile device.
  • Build Custom Apps: Create personalized productivity apps tailored to your needs.

Why Swiftian?

In a world where coding education meets practical app development, Swiftian offers a streamlined, accessible experience. It follows the familiar Swift language syntax, ensuring that you learn best practices while experimenting with a mini IDE concept that simplifies app creation.

Getting Started
