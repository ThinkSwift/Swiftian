Privacy Policy

Effective June 28, 2026

This policy applies to the apps published by Swiftian (“we,” “us”). We keep things simple: our apps are built to work without accounts, and we do not collect, sell, or share personal information for advertising.

What we collect

Our apps work without you giving us your name, email, or any personal details. We don’t maintain our own user accounts or tracking servers.

iCloud & shared content

Some apps let you save and share content (for example, worlds you create). When you do, that content is stored in Apple’s iCloud / CloudKit under your Apple ID and handled according to Apple’s Privacy Policy. We can’t see your Apple ID, and we only process the content you choose to publish in order to make features like sharing and remixing work.

Third-party services

Apps distributed through the App Store rely on Apple for delivery, payments, and basic, aggregated analytics. We don’t embed third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs.

Children

Our apps are family-friendly and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as our apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
